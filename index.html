About

Dr. Nana O. Sarpong is an orthopedic surgeon specializing in hip and knee arthroplasty. His clinical and research work focuses on modern joint replacement techniques, revision arthroplasty, and optimizing patient outcomes through data-driven care.

He is actively involved in academic research exploring surgical innovation, implant technology, and patient-reported outcomes following joint replacement.

  • Total Knee Arthroplasty
  • Revision Hip Replacement
  • Revision Knee Replacement
  • Direct Anterior Hip Replacement
  • Robotic-Assisted Joint Replacement

Research

Dr. Sarpong has published research on hip and knee arthroplasty, implant design, surgical approaches, and patient outcomes. His work appears in peer-reviewed orthopedic journals and national orthopedic conferences.
